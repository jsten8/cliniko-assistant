How This App Works
The big picture

Cliniko Assistant does the admin work that used to take you a long time to do manually. It connects to your Cliniko account, finds the patients you need to action, reads the relevant documents, fills out your Word templates automatically, and handles the emails — so you can focus on patients instead of paperwork.

Think of it as a smart assistant that knows which patients need follow-up letters sent and walks you through each one step by step.

What happens each time you open the app
Good to know
🔒
Patient files are never saved to your computer
The app reads PDFs directly from Cliniko without downloading them. Your patient data stays in Cliniko where it belongs.
🕐
The app remembers what you've already done
Patients you've actioned stay greyed out in the worklist even if you close and reopen the app.
📝
You're always in control
The app makes suggestions — recommended files, auto-detected workflow types, pre-filled fields — but you can change anything before anything gets sent.
⚙️
Templates and settings are easy to update
If your Word templates change, just go to Settings and upload the new version.
